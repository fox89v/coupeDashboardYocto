# Support Open Coupé Lab — Let’s build together the open-source lab for the Fiat Coupé

**Official English Translation – Open Coupé Lab (November 2025)**

## What is Open Coupé Lab
Open Coupé Lab is a non-profit initiative created by enthusiasts of electronics, software, and classic cars.  
Its goal is to build a technical lab for open-source research and development of digital dashboards and control electronics for the Fiat Coupé and other historic vehicles.

Everything we make — hardware, software, 3D designs, documentation — will be free and public.
